Privacy Policy (${appNameEn})

Last updated: ${lastUpdated}

We value your privacy. This app helps you break down visions into actionable daily steps. The following explains how we handle data.

Information We Collect

  • Account identifier generated by Sign in with Apple (no email or real name).
  • Content you enter in the app: goals, tasks, chat messages, daily mood/reflection, used to provide core features.

How We Use Information

  • Generate goal decomposition and daily action suggestions.
  • Sync your plans and progress across devices and cloud storage.
  • Maintain stability and security (basic logs without sensitive content).

AI Notice

AI-generated content is only used to help users break down their vision into actionable daily steps and is not professional advice. Please use your judgment.

To generate responses, your input is sent to a third-party AI service provider solely for response generation.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase for authentication and data storage.
  • A third-party AI service provider for response generation.

Data Retention

Your account data and content are retained until you delete your account and data in the app. System logs are kept for up to 30 days for stability and security.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and data in the app. Deletion is irreversible.

Global Availability

This app is available globally. We comply with applicable data rights in your region.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: ${supportEmail}

隐私政策（${appNameZh} / ${appNameEn}）

最后更新：${lastUpdated}

我们重视你的隐私。本应用用于帮助你将愿景拆解为可执行的每日行动。以下说明我们如何处理数据。

我们收集哪些信息

  • 账号标识：通过“Sign in with Apple”生成的用户标识符（不包含邮箱与真实姓名）。
  • 你在应用内输入的内容：目标、任务、对话内容、每日心情/反思等，用于提供核心功能。

我们如何使用信息

  • 生成愿景拆解与每日行动建议。
  • 在你的设备与云端同步你的计划与进度。
  • 改善应用体验与稳定性（必要的基础日志，不包含敏感内容）。

AI 使用说明

AI 生成内容仅用于帮助用户将愿景拆解为可执行的每日行动，不构成专业建议。请结合自身情况判断与使用。

为了生成回复，你的输入会被发送给第三方 AI 服务提供方进行处理，仅用于回复生成。

第三方服务

  • Supabase：用于认证与数据存储。
  • 第三方 AI 服务提供方：用于生成对话回复。

数据保留

你的账号数据与内容将保留至你在应用内删除账号与数据为止。系统日志最长保留 30 天，用于稳定性与安全性分析。

数据删除

你可以在应用内选择“删除账号与数据”，删除后无法恢复。

面向全球用户

本应用面向全球发布。若你所在地区的法律赋予你更多数据权利，我们将依法配合。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：${supportEmail}
